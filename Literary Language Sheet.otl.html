Literary Language Terms

  1. Allegory
    1. Definition

      A discription of something using other images (Animal Farm)

    2. Example

      A man is like a pig, alway hungry

    Alliteration

    1. Definition

      A repetition of 2 words

    2. Example

      Behemoth, biggest born of earth, upheaved His vastness. --Milton.

    Allusion

    1. Definition

      Something not directly mentioned/seen, usually done on purpose

    2. Example

      He was seen (who is he, now you could make asumptions by what was said previously)

    Ambiguity

    1. Definition

      Not an exact definition

    2. Example

      Red(what shade of red?)
